Explore ICT Career Paths

Discover top roles in Information and Communication Technology with real impact and benefits.

Computer and Information Systems Manager

Oversees all computer-related activities in an organization, including infrastructure, data, and networks.

Benefits:
  • High salary potential
  • Leadership opportunities
  • High-demand across industries

Computer Support Specialist

Provides help and technical assistance to individuals and organizations using computer software or equipment.

Benefits:
  • Entry-level access to ICT field
  • Good job stability
  • Flexible work environments

Database Analyst

Designs, tests, and manages data systems to store and organize large sets of information efficiently and securely.

Benefits:
  • Excellent data literacy
  • Works with big data technologies
  • Critical for business decisions

Digital Strategist

Plans and executes digital campaigns, combining data and creativity to grow brand presence online.

Benefits:
  • Creative and analytical skills
  • Work in marketing, media, or startups
  • Fast-growing digital field

Information Security Analyst

Protects computer systems and networks from security breaches and cyberattacks.

Benefits:
  • Highly in-demand role
  • Vital to all organizations
  • Lucrative salaries

Mobilization Specialist

Coordinates the deployment of tech teams or systems across locations for project implementation or support.

Benefits:
  • Hands-on role in large-scale projects
  • Opportunities to travel
  • Team coordination experience

Privacy Coordinator

Ensures that personal and organizational data is handled in compliance with privacy regulations and best practices.

Benefits:
  • Legal and tech skill combination
  • Important in healthcare, finance, and tech
  • Focus on data protection

Software Engineer

Designs, develops, tests, and maintains software systems for various industries and platforms.

Benefits:
  • Creative problem solving
  • Remote work flexibility
  • Top-paying job in ICT

Web Developer

Builds and maintains websites and web apps, working with code, design, and user experience.

Benefits:
  • Highly creative and in demand
  • Freelance or in-house options
  • Endless specialization paths

Web Marketing Manager

Leads online marketing campaigns to drive traffic, engagement, and sales through digital platforms.

Benefits:
  • Work with SEO, analytics, and content
  • Oversee teams and strategy
  • Fast-growing marketing sector
